SUSA Timeline

  1. 2009: The Beginning of SUSA

    SUSA, originally called USA, was established by five students in 2009.

  2. 2010 - 2014: The First Events

    Hosted first statistic major hangout in 2010 and established committees in 2014.

  3. 2014: Structural Changes and Growth

    When the club first started, there was just one executive committee. It hosted sessions on how to choose statistics classes for future semesters and also hosted movie nights on "math movies" such as Moneyball and Beautiful Mind. The club was first started to facilitate more social events for statistics students. The club started with three committees: Events, Project, and Marketing. Afterwards, a competition committee (Kaggle) was added.

  4. 2015: Hosted Academic and Networking Events

    Hosted faculty events, invited professors as club advisors, and hosted career talks.

  5. 2017: Club Name Change

    Originally called USA (Undergraduate Statistics Association), the club then changed its name to SUSA (Statistics Undergraduate Students Association).

  6. 2018: Now

    From five students starting SUSA to having about 10 committed members in 2013, we had 100 total members in Fall 2017, and we now have 160 in Fall 2018.
